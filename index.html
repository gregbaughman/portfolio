About Me

I am currently a senior at Iowa State University, where I am pursuing my bachelors of science in Aerospace Engineering. My journey at ISU has been enriched by my involvement in the Greek community through my fraternity, as well as my active participation in the Iowa State Weight Club and SALT ministries. I also serve as a peer mentor in the Aerospace Department, where I have the privilege of guiding and supporting the freshman class.

In my free time, I find great joy in building and flying radio-controlled FPV systems as well as exploring data analytics, particularly in the context of algorithmic and quantitative trading. I am driven by my passion for systems and structural engineering, and as I approach the end of my academic career, I am excited to embark on a professional journey in engineering where I hope to contribute to advancements in my specific field and explore new opportunities in and out of my career.
